Servo Air

The Servo Air Ventilator provides clinically accurate ventilation with user-friendly controls and advanced lung protection. Its turbine-driven system allows autonomy from a central gas supply, while its 120-minute battery life supports intra-hospital transport. Cost-effective and low-maintenance, it ensures precise care with minimal operational costs in any clinical setting.
